About

Garrett Crabb is a producer with fifteen years of experience turning ideas into finished work — across brand campaigns, product launches, competitive work, and everything in between.

Trained as a painter at MICA, he came to production not through design but through a genuine love of the process: the problem-solving, the collaboration, the hidden labor of making something difficult look inevitable.

He's worked at Ogilvy, Friendshop, and Something Different, where for nearly a decade he's served as Special Ops and Head of Production.

The most interesting stories about this work are the ones I can't tell you — the pivots, the deals, the last-minute ingenuity that made something possible that probably shouldn't have been. Every project has a story. Most of them can't be published.
